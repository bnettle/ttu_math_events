{\LARGE \textbf{ABSTRACT.}}
{\Large
We study spatially periodic functions of Gevrey classes. 
These functions are analytic everywhere with a uniform radius of analyticity, and their norms are stronger than all Sobolev ones.
Basic estimates for solutions of the Navier-Stokes equations in Gevrey spaces will be proved.
Applications to asymptotic expansions for Leray-Hopf weak solutions will be demonstrated.
Strong results with simple calculations are obtained by using the celebrated Foias-Temam techniques.
