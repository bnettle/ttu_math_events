The XVII Red Raider Mini-Symposium is entitledCurrent Trends in Numerical Analysis and Scientific Computingand is featuring five distinguished speakers and five early-career speakers. The goal of the minisymposium is tobring together top scientists in the areas of Numerical Analysis and Scientific Computing to understand the mostimportant lines of investigation in these fields. The minisymposium features an interplay of both theoretical andpractical aspects that are at the forefront of current research efforts in the scientific community.
