{\LARGE \textbf{ABSTRACT.}}
{\Large
We study the long-time behavior of solutions to the three-dimensional Navier-Stokes equations with periodic boundary
conditions. The asymptotic expansions  for all Leray-Hopf weak solutions
are obtained. We show that if the force has an asymptotic expansion, as time goes to infinity, in terms of negative-power
functions in Sobolev-Gevrey space, then any weak solution admits an asymptotic expansion of the same type. This is a joint work with Luan Hoang (Texas Tech University).
}
